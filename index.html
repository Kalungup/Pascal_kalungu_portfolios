Professional Summary

Bridging Project Management, Artificial Intelligence, Data Science and Information Systems to deliver intelligent solutions with measurable real-world impact.

I am Pascal Kalungu, a Data Analyst, Data Scientist, and Project Manager with over 8+ years of experience leading projects across international development, digital transformation, and organizational capacity building.

Throughout my career as a Project Manager, I recognized that successful projects require more than strong planning and execution—they demand a deep understanding of technology, systems architecture, and the strategic use of data. As organizations increasingly rely on data-driven decision-making, I developed a passion for bridging the gap between business strategy and technology.

This journey led me to pursue a Master's in Strategic Information Systems Management at Stockholm University under the prestigious Swedish Institute (SI) Scholarship for Global Professionals . The programme has equipped me with advanced knowledge in enterprise and systems architecture, business process analysis, requirements engineering, data modelling, business intelligence, big data platforms, cloud technologies, and AI-driven analytics.

Today, I combine project management expertise with data science and information systems to design solutions that align business needs with technology. My goal is to help organizations make smarter decisions, optimize processes, and deliver sustainable, data-driven innovation that creates measurable impact.
